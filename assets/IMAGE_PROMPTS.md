# Bild-Prompts — Enigma-Challenge (4 Bilder, Gemini-App / Google AI Pro)

**So vorgehen (für Stil-Konsistenz wichtig):**
1. Alle 4 Bilder in **EINER** Gemini-App-Unterhaltung erzeugen. Bild 1 (Title) zuerst — es setzt den Look.
2. Für Bild 2–4 davor schreiben: *"Same visual style, palette, grain and mood as the previous image. Now:"* + den jeweiligen Prompt. So hält die App den Stil über den Kontext.
3. Wenn die App ein Seitenverhältnis anbietet: **16:9**.
4. Pro Bild **eine** gute Ausgabe wählen, nicht in Re-Roll-Schleifen verlieren (Konzil-Regel: max. 4, kein ästhetisches Re-Roll).
5. Speichern unter `game/assets/` mit exакt diesen Namen: `title.jpg`, `signature.jpg`, `terminal-bg.jpg`, `win.jpg` (png/webp auch ok — dann Namen entsprechend).
6. Kurz notieren, **welches Modell** die App benutzt hat (für den Erfahrungsbericht / Google-AI-Bonus).
7. Der **Lose-Zustand** braucht KEIN Bild (wird per CSS als Blackout gelöst).

## Gemeinsamer STIL-Block (steht sinngemäß in jedem Bild)

> Cinematic, moody, near-black background. Cold cyan-green CRT/phosphor glow as the only cool light. A single faint warm amber light low on the horizon (the solstice sun). Fine film grain and subtle CRT scanlines. Aesthetic: a 1940s Bletchley Park codebreaking hut fused with abstract emergent computation; schematic-meets-photoreal; very high contrast, lots of negative space. NO text, NO letters or numbers, NO logos, NO watermark, NO human figures, NO faces, NO eyes, NO creatures, NO spaceship, NO rainbow. 16:9.

---

## 1 — `title.jpg` (Title / Key Art)
> [STIL-Block] A dark codebreaking desk in a dim hut: an old electromechanical cipher machine half-dissolving upward into a vast lattice of light that rises behind it like an aurora made of pure computation — geometric, self-similar, cold cyan-green. A thin band of warm amber dawn light along the bottom horizon. The feeling is of an intelligence emerging from the structure of the machine itself, not arriving from outside. Wide cinematic key art, lots of dark negative space at the top for a title.

## 2 — `signature.jpg` (Die fremde Intelligenz / „die KI der Menschheit"-Gegenüber)
> [STIL-Block] An abstract visualization of a non-human intelligence that has emerged from computation itself: a colossal, self-consistent standing-wave / interference pattern of fine cyan-green filaments folding into an impossible lattice — calm, patient, watchful, but with NO face, NO eyes, NO body. It must read as a *structure*, not a creature. Centered, near-symmetrical, deep black surround, a faint amber undertone far behind it.

## 3 — `terminal-bg.jpg` (Hintergrund-Plate hinter dem grünen Terminal-Text)
> [STIL-Block] A very dark, low-contrast background plate for a computer terminal: a dim Bletchley-hut interior reduced to suggestion — faint phosphor glow, soft scanlines, blurred bokeh of indicator lights, and mostly EMPTY near-black space in the centre so bright text can be overlaid on top. Subtle, unobtrusive, no focal subject, darker and flatter than the other images.

## 4 — `win.jpg` (Auflösung / Sieg — die Sonnenwende kippt zurück)
> [STIL-Block] The resolution moment after a long night: the warm amber solstice sun breaks gently over a calm horizon while the cold cyan-green computational lattice powers down and harmonises into the warm light — tension releasing, the days about to grow longer again. Hopeful but restrained and serious, NOT saccharine. Wide cinematic, balanced composition.
